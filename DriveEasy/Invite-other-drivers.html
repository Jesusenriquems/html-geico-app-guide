Invite Other Drivers

Open the GEICO app and tap on the "Login" button to sign in with the policyholder’s credentials.

Once accessed to the GEICO app go to the top right corner to the settings symbol button.

Once accessed to the settings screen, proceed to the "DriveEasy" section.

Access to the "Driver Profiles"option to access to other drivers information for driveEasy.

In this page the PH will see all the drivers that can be invited to their driveEasy program, advise them to click on the option "invite" next to the name of the desired driver.

Once selected the driver to invite confirm the phone number to send invitation (make sure is the phone number registered in telematics in order to be accepted in the program).

This is the last step!. Advise ph that one pop-up screen / sms screen will show and is to send the invitation to the desired driver, once this step has been completed the desired driver to enroll needs to open this link to complete the enrollment process.
