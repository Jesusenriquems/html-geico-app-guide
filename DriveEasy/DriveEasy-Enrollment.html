DriveEasy Enrollment

BEFORE login with credentials advise PH to click in the option under the login credentials marked with a steering weels with the name "DriveEasy".

Advise PH to introduce the phone number they used to register in DriveEasy.

PH MUST accept the location permition FIRST in order to proceed with other permitions and advise them to make sure and accept the "precise location".

Once the location tracking has been allowed, advise PH to accept remaining permitions that appear on their screen in order to successfully enroll in DriveEasy.

If applyPH at this point, PH will be require to type the phone numbers of other drivers listed on the policy that were added to the DriveEasy program.

Congratulations!, you have completed the enrollment process, now you can advise the PH that after they see this screen below on their phone, they can access now to their GEICO App and they will be able to see their driveEasy Dashboard once they login!.
