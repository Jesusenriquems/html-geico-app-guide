Change Role in DriveEasy

Open the GEICO app and tap on the "Login" button to sign in with the policyholder’s credentials.

(If applicable) Advise customer after they login to select their auto policy to in order to access to change the trip Status

Advise PH that after they get into their Auto Policy, they must get into the DriveEasy Dashboard (they will identify it under the score with blue letters)

Once PH has access to the DriveEasy Dashboard advise them to go to the most recent trip / trip they want to update and advise them to go to "View Trip Details"

After PH locates their desired trip to update and access to the trip details they will see the option available named "Change Role", advise them to click in there.

Ask PH to select the desired role they want to apply to the trip. (Most likely they want to switch to passenger)

(If applicable) Advise customer if asked, to accept the changes for that trip.

You made it!, after you complete the previous step, the PH will be able to see the updated status for that trip.
