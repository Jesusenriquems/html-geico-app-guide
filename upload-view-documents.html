View and Upload Documents

Open the GEICO app and tap on the "Login" button to sign in with the policyholder’s credentials.

Click in the "Policy" symbol on the bottom of the screen.

Once ph is on the policy page, advise to click on the option "policy Documents".

At this point, the policyholder will see two options (view documents or upload documents).

By clicking in the "View Documents" button the policyholder will be able to see multiple Documents such as the ones shown in the image below.
