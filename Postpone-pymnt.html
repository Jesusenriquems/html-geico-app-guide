Postpone Payment

Open the GEICO app and tap on the "Login" button to sign in with the policyholder’s credentials.

Access to the billing screen by going to the money symbol on the bottom of the screen.

Select the option "Make changes to your upcoming Auto Pay payment"
(Note: see in example the current due date to compare with final step once process is complete).

Select the option "Change Your Auto Pay Schedule Date" to access the desired date to change the date of next payment.

Select the calendar symbol to access the available dates to postpone payment (advise ph they can only postpone up to 9 days after their due date).


Select the option "Continue" to accept the change of due date for next payment.

That's it!, confirm with ph that their due date has changed to the new date.
