GEICO App timeline

Open the GEICO app and tap on the "Login" button to sign in with the policyholder’s credentials.

Click in the "Policy" symbol on the bottom of the screen.

Once ph is on the policy page, advise to click on the option "Account History" for them to access to their timeline.

At this point, the policyholder will see their timeline and fine multiple information such as:
- Time of Policy History.
- Transaction Type.
- The date and time of the Transaction.
- Title and description of transaction itself.

By clicking in the "Transaction Type" button the policyholder will be able to filter by the type of transaction they want to verify.
(Note: This timeline from the GEICO app will NOT show policy notes or other changes that are only vissible for agents such as UW changes.)
